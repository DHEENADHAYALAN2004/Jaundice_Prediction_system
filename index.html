What is Jaundice?

Jaundice is a condition that causes yellowing of the skin and whites of the eyes due to high levels of bilirubin in the blood.

Common Symptoms

  • Yellowing of skin and eyes
  • Dark urine
  • Pale stools
  • Fatigue

Treatment Options

  • Phototherapy
  • Treating underlying conditions
  • Lifestyle changes
  • Regular monitoring
